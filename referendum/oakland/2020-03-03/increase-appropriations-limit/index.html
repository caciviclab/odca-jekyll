Measure S

Increase Appropriations Limit

Summary

This measure would authorize the City of Oakland to continue spending the proceeds of voter-approved City taxes, including Measure M (Emergency Medical Services), Measure N (Paramedic Services), Measure Q (Library Services), Measure C (Hotel Tax), Measure Z (Public Safety and Violence Prevention Services), Measure HH (Sugar Sweetened Beverages), Measure D (Public Libraries Preservation), Measure W (Vacant Properties), as well as other taxes received by the City such as, Property Tax, State Tax, Business License Tax, Utility Consumption Tax, Real Estate Transfer Tax, Transient Occupancy Tax, and the Parking Tax.
